## Nhom san pham Synco nen tu van
- Intercom/headset: Xtalk S, Xtalk X, Xtalk XPro, Xtalk XMax, Xtalk Master, Xtalk Link, CE3 tally light.
- Micro khong day: G series, P series cho dien thoai, Smic, PX, G1/G2/G3/G4, micro lavalier.
- Broadcast/on-camera: D series, M series, S series, V series.
- Phone monitor/video: XView M4, XView M4 Touch, XView M4 AI, XVision HD.

## Diem tu van nhanh
- Xtalk Series phu hop ekip quay phim, livestream, su kien, san khau can lien lac hai chieu khong day.
- Xtalk X ho tro nhom nho; Xtalk XPro/XMax huong den nhom lon hon; Xtalk Master huong den giao tiep chuyen nghiep, ket noi master-free, quan ly bang app.
- Theo thong tin hang, Xtalk Master co 2.4GHz, full-duplex, tam xa LOS 500m, do tre khoang 45ms, pin khoang 12 gio va sac USB-C. Neu khach can thong so chinh xac theo phien ban, de nghi nhan vien kiem tra model.
- Xtalk S co thiet ke in-ear/neckband, phu hop nhu cau deo gon nhe va lien lac nhom nho.
- P series phu hop creator quay bang dien thoai; can hoi khach dung iPhone Lightning, USB-C hay thiet bi khac truoc khi goi y.
- Micro khong day can hoi muc dich: phong van, livestream, vlog, quay su kien, so nguoi noi, khoang cach va thiet bi nhan am thanh.
