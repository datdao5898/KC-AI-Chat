# Chinh sach va pham vi tu van KingCom

Nguon tham khao cong khai:
- https://store.kingcom.com.vn/pages/chinh-sach-bao-hanh
- https://store.kingcom.com.vn/pages/chinh-sach-doi-tra
- https://newlite.vn/pages/chinh-sach-doi-tra

## Gia va VAT
- Gia niem yet cua tat ca san pham NewLite da bao gom VAT (full VAT). Khach khong can tra them VAT khi mua hang.
- NewLite ho tro xuat hoa don VAT theo thong tin don hang hop le.

## Pham vi bat buoc
- Chi tu van theo du lieu trong products.csv va kien thuc training cua nguon dang nhan tin.
- Khong bia gia, ton kho, khuyen mai, thoi gian giao hang, thoi han bao hanh rieng cua tung model neu du lieu khong co.
- Neu can xac nhan thong tin thuong mai, xin model/SKU va so dien thoai de nhan vien KingCom kiem tra.
- Neu co xung dot giua file chung va file rieng fanpage/website, uu tien file rieng cua nguon dang chat.

## Chinh sach bao hanh
- Chinh sach KingCom cong khai neu: than may thuong 12 thang + 1 thang, phu kien 3 thang + 1 thang, tuy tung loai san pham.
- Dieu kien bao hanh: tem bao hanh con ro, phieu bao hanh/hang hoa hop le, hoa don ro rang, loi xuat phat tu chat luong san pham.
- Cac truong hop khong bao hanh mien phi: loi do nguoi dung, thien tai/tai nan, am uot/hao mon, dong vat xam nhap, san pham khong chinh hang, sua chua ngoai trung tam chi dinh, IMEI khong khop, tem rach/tay xoa, hao mon tu nhien.
- Khach can sao luu du lieu truoc khi gui thiet bi bao hanh neu san pham co du lieu/phan mem.
- Doi moi trong 7 ngay neu san pham, bao bi, phu kien con moi 100%, co bang chung mua tu KingCom va loi duoc QA xac nhan la loi tu than san pham.

## Chinh sach doi tra
- Doi tra khi hang khong dung chung loai/mau ma nhu don dat, khong du so luong/khong du bo, hoac bi anh huong ben ngoai khi nhan hang.
- Truong hop thieu phu kien/qua tang/be vo: khach can thong bao trong 48 gio tu khi nhan san pham.
- Thoi gian gui tra theo chinh sach cong khai: trong 3 ngay tu khi nhan san pham.
- Khach can co chung tu/hinh anh/thong tin don hang de KingCom hoan tat doi tra.

## Van chuyen va ho tro
- KingCom ho tro giao hang toan quoc, nhung khong tu khang dinh phi van chuyen/thoi gian giao chinh xac neu chua co dia chi va don hang.
- San pham con han bao hanh: KingCom chi tra chi phi gui tra san pham sau bao hanh theo chinh sach cong khai.
- San pham het han bao hanh: khach chiu chi phi van chuyen va chi phi phat sinh neu co.

## Cach noi voi khach
- Neu khach hoi chinh sach: tra loi ngan gon theo cac muc tren va khuyen khach cung cap model/SKU/don hang de nhan vien kiem tra chinh xac.
- Neu khach can xu ly bao hanh/doi tra: xin so dien thoai, model, mo ta loi, thoi diem mua va anh/hinh tinh trang san pham neu co.
